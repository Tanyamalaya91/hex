# Plugins

Plugins are used for basic features of Hex and provide a mechanism to extend functionality. The hex-provided plugins are listed below. Note: when a plugin is used in your rules, they will be automatically downloaded on startup, or you can place them in your Plugins Directory.

- [local](https://github.com/mmcquillan/hex-local)
- [response](https://github.com/mmcquillan/hex-response)
- [ssh](https://github.com/mmcquillan/hex-ssh)
- [twilio](https://github.com/mmcquillan/hex-twilio)
- [validate](https://github.com/mmcquillan/hex-validate)
- [winrm](https://github.com/mmcquillan/hex-winrm)
